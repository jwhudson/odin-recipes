Chicken Tikka Masala

Description

Chicken tikka masala is a classic curried dish with soft tender chunks of char-grilled chicken, simmered briefly in a delicious and aromatic tikka masala sauce.

Ingredients

Marinade
  • 1 teaspoon red chilli powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon coriander
  • 1/4 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon Oil
  • 1/2 cup Greek Yoghurt
Sauce
  • Onions
  • Chilli
  • Garlic
  • Tomato Puree
  • Coriander
  • Cumin
  • Garam Masala

Steps

  1. Marinade chicken 12 hours
  2. Saute onions
  3. Add spices
  4. Add tomato puree
  5. Grill chicken pieces
  6. Add Yoghurt to sauce
  7. Add Chicken to sauce
  8. Serve with rice
