Tacos

A simple tasty classic

Description

Quick to prepare, high protein turkey tacos.

Ingredients

  • Turkey mince
  • Tortillas or taco shells
  • Red onion
  • Red pepper
  • Guac
  • Salsa
  • Sweetcorn
  • Mixed taco beans
  • Smokey tomato sauce

Steps

  1. Brown the turkey mince
  2. Add taco seasoning
  3. Add beans and sauce
  4. Dice veg
  5. Serve
